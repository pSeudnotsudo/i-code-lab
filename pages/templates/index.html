Our Programs

Hands-on in-person classes designed to spark curiosity and build real tech skills

All Levels Ages 7–18

Coding

From Scratch to Python and beyond — kids learn to think like programmers and build their own apps, games, and websites.

Hands-On Ages 8–18

Robotics

Design, build, and program real robots. Students develop engineering and problem-solving skills through hands-on projects.

Intermediate Ages 10–18

Cybersecurity

Learn how to stay safe online and understand ethical hacking, networks, and digital defence fundamentals.

All Levels Ages 9–18

Drone Training

Pilot, program, and build drones. Students explore aeronautics and autonomous systems in a safe, guided environment.

Advanced Ages 12–18

Artificial Intelligence

Explore machine learning, neural networks, and AI tools — demystifying the technology shaping tomorrow's world.

In-Person Weekdays & Weekends

Flexible Schedules

After-school programs, weekend bootcamps, and holiday camps. Open Mon–Fri 8:30am–8:30pm and weekends 8:30am–4pm.
