Who We Are

What is i-CODE?

i-CODE is a tech generation studio that interfaces from a basic to an advanced level — cultivating talent through unique, hands-on learning in Robotics, AI, Drones, Coding and STEM. Based at One-Stop Arcade, Karen, Nairobi, we are a project-based lab built to produce Africa's next wave of innovators.

🎯

Our Mission

To democratise access to world-class technology education in Africa — providing every learner with the practical skills, mentorship and community needed to build real solutions and lead in the digital economy.

🌍

Our Vision

To be Africa's most impactful tech innovation lab — a place where the next generation of engineers, AI researchers, robotics builders and digital entrepreneurs are born, trained and launched into the world.
